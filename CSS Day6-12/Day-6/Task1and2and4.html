PlayerUnknowns Battlegrounds (PUBG) is one of the most popular battle royale games in the world. developed and published by PUBG Corporation, a subsidiary of Bluehole Studio. Released in 2017, the game revolutionized the online multiplayer genre by introducing large-scale survival gameplay where up to 100 players. parachute onto an island, scavenge for weapons and equipment, and fight to be the last person or team standing. The shrinking safe zone mechanic adds tension and forces players into combat, making every match unpredictable and exciting.
