SOCIAL

Social life is an important part of human living. It refers to the way people interact, communicate, and build relationships with others in society. Through social activities, people share ideas, help each other, and develop a sense of belonging. Being social helps improve communication skills, teamwork, and understanding among individuals. It also plays a key role in emotional well-being, as it allows people to express themselves and form meaningful connections in their community.

BIO DIVERSITY

India is a megadiverse country, a term employed for 17 countries that display high biological diversity and contain many species exclusively indigenous, or endemic, to them. India is the habitat for 8.6% of all mammals, 13.7% of bird species, 7.9% of reptile species, 6% of amphibian species, 12.2% of fish species, and 6.0% of all flowering plant species. Fully a third of Indian plant species are endemic.
