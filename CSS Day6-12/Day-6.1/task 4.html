COIMBATORE

Coimbatore, often called the “Manchester of South India,” is a major city in Tamil Nadu known for its strong industrial base and pleasant climate. It is famous for its textile industry, engineering firms, and educational institutions

The city lies on the banks of the Noyyal River and is surrounded by the Western Ghats, which provide beautiful scenery and a comfortable environment throughout the year.

Apart from industries, Coimbatore is also a growing hub for IT and startups. It is known for its traditional culture mixed with modern development.
